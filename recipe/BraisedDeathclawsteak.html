Braised deathclaw steak

A high fat meat that is filled with layer by layer of protien. Refeashingly keeping you full all day while wondering the wasteland.

Ingredients

  1. 2 1/4 pounds of DeathClaw meat
  2. 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  3. 1/3 cup of water
  4. 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  5. 1 cup of soysauce
  6. 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  7. 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  8. 1/2 cups chicken broth
  9. One 3-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled.
  10. 3 garlic cloves
  11. 2 lemon grass stalks
  12. 3 bay leaves
  13. 3 star anise
  14. 3 cardamom pods
  15. 1 cinnammon stick
  16. 2 dried chiles de arbol (or other pepers )
  17. 1 tablespoon peppercorns

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees farienheight. Place the pork belly in a large pot and cover with cold water. over a medium-high heat. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 15 minuets. Remove any foam that floats to the top. Remove the pork belly from the water and carefully cut into bite-sized peices.
  2. Add the granulated sugar, light brown sugar, and 1/3 cup water to a large, oven proof pot and place over medium-high heat.Wisk together untill the sugar has dissovled, then add the pork belly to the pot and stir to coat. Add all the remaining Ingredients to the pot and bring to a low boil.
  3. Cover the pot with an ovenproof lid and transfer to the oven.Bake for 1 hour. Increase the heat of the oven to 375 degrees and remove the cover. Bake for 1 hour more, stiring the contents ever 15 minuets
  4. Remove the pot from the oven and let it rest on the stove. Takea cup of the sauce from the pot and pour into a small saucepan over high heat.Cook untill the sauce reduces and thickens.Toss the pork peices in the sauce and serve.
