Insta-Mash

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of russet potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 fresh rosemary sprig
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of blue cheese
  • 1 teaspoon fresh parsley minced then measured
  • salt and peper

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water add the potatoes bay leaf,rosemary sprig, and salt. Bring to boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes, untill the potatoes are tender
  2. Drain the water and remove the bay leaf and rosemary sprig. Place the pot back on the stove over low heat and add the butter and sour cream. Mash the potatoes untill smooth. Add the blu cheese and parsley and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
